Getting Started
Below, you will find helpful information on how to Decraft and Craft items.
Decrafting
You can decraft items that you don't want into Raw Material.
  1. Click the Decraft Items button, under Tools.
  2. Select the items that you want to decraft from the inventory window on the left.
  3. Items you choose will be removed from your inventory and go into the window on the right.
  4. Once you have selected the items you want to decraft, click Decraft.

    5. That's it! Your items have now been removed from your inventory and decrafted into Raw Material, which is stored in this crafting table.
Basic Crafting
You can use Raw Material to craft new items.
  1. You can Decraft items into Raw Material. Select the items that you want to Decraft, then click the Decraft button.

    These items will be removed from your inventory and Raw Material will be added to the Crafting Table.
Advanced Crafting
This section is only recommended for experienced players.

Some items are so complex that they require Raw Material and other items in order to be crafted.
