What is loot?

Loot is all the items that you can find inside of loot boxes. Loot boxes are small containers on the ground that contain different types of loot.

Looking for better loot? Look harder! Better loot is more difficult to obtain because the loot boxes are more difficult to find.

How do I know if my loot is good?

Each item that you obtain is part of a certain tier of loot. There are 5 tiers of loot, as explained below. Some items are still good if they are lower tier, because they may have interesting uses.

Common Tier

These items are the lowest tier of items and are very easy to find. They can be found in Tier 1 and Tier 2 loot boxes.

Uncommon Tier

These items are the second lowest tier of items and are easy to find. They can be found in Tier 1 and Tier 2 loot boxes.

Rare Tier

These items are the middle tier of items and are moderately difficult to find. They can be found in Tier 2 and Tier 3 loot boxes.

Epic Tier

These items are the second highest tier of items and are very difficult to find. They can only be found in Tier 3 loot boxes.

Legendary Tier

These items are the highest tier of items and are nearly impossible to obtain. They can only be obtained through Airdrops.

Where do I find loot?

Loot is found all over the place, but mostly in settlements, such as cities. It's hidden in corners, wedged in objects, and concealed on tops of things.

Better loot is harder to find. You'll find all sorts of Tier 1 loot on the ground in easy to spot places.

When a loot box is opened, it will respawn in a certain amount of time, usually between a half hour to a few hours. Higher tiers of loot boxes take longer to respawn.

What kinds of loot boxes are there?

There are three tiers of loot boxes: Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3.
